Ezi.Zhang
Based in Shanghai

Ezi.Zhang is a versatile artist whose primary creative mediums include mixed-media painting, printmaking, 3D, generative art, and sound. In the process of absorbing various types of information, Ezi collects elements that trigger their synesthesia as pigments, and then engages in re-creation within the realm of soul memory. This is the process of Ezi's self and its visualization.
