STILL FRAMES 影像静帧

I seek to explore the relationship between the digital world and perception, presenting this exploration through the medium of moving images.

Rasterize – Shining is not only an imagination of the future but also a deeper inquiry into contemporary self-awareness: in a world governed by data and virtual environments, how does the individual rediscover the self amid loss and chaos? The interweaving of sound, imagery, and color within the work constructs a sensory experience — a recreation of a "digital myth."

In the creative process, I combined generative art with sound design, treating each design element as a vessel of emotion and belief. Together, they shape an immersive space suspended between reality and the virtual, where E is gradually drawn into forces that are both mysterious and unknown.

This work explores how, in the digital age, humans forge new connections among emotion, perception, imagery, information, and belief, while seeking to reveal the deep interweaving of the digital and the emotional within the realm of perception. 我试图探索数字世界与感知之间的关系，通过动态影像这一媒介来呈现这种探索。

光栅化——闪耀不仅是对未来的想象，更是对当代自我意识的深层探究：在由数据和虚拟环境主导的世界中，个体如何在迷失与混乱中重新发现自我？作品中声音、影像与色彩的交织构建了一种感官体验——一种"数字神话"的再创造。

在创作过程中，我将生成艺术与声音设计相结合，将每个设计元素视为情感和信仰的载体。它们共同塑造了一个悬浮在现实与虚拟之间的沉浸式空间，E逐渐被吸引到既神秘又未知的力量中。

这件作品探索了在数字时代，人类如何在情感、感知、影像、信息和信仰之间建立新的联系，同时试图揭示感知领域中数字与情感的深层交织。

Artwork Story Synopsis 作品故事概要

In a future world dominated by data and technology, E is a data designer exploring the hidden connections between digital information and emotions within an ever-changing virtual space. As the analysis deepens, E realizes that data is not just a cold symbol; it carries emotions and life within it.

These images and sounds weave together to form a completely new world, breaking the boundaries between the physical and virtual, almost like a redefinition of the past world. Through this intertwining of data and emotions, E gradually transforms from an observer to a part of this world, becoming the eternal link between the virtual realm and human emotions. 在一个由数据和技术主导的未来世界中，E是一位数据设计师，在瞬息万变的虚拟空间中探索数字信息与情感之间的隐藏联系。当他意外发现3540张被遗忘的图像时，他将它们输入系统，并通过音频频率解码它们的潜在联系。随着分析的深入，E意识到数据不仅仅是冰冷的符号；它承载着情感和生命。

这些图像和声音交织在一起，形成了一个全新的世界，打破了物理与虚拟之间的界限，几乎像是对过去世界的重新定义。通过数据与情感的这种交织，E逐渐从观察者转变为这个世界的一部分，成为虚拟领域与人类情感之间的永恒纽带。

In a world increasingly reconstructed by information, the human individual resembles a brainless slime mold—navigating through fragments of perception and data flows, always seeking the shortest path to survival.

My work Rasterize: Consciousness of the Digital Myxomycete takes the slime mold as a model to simulate a "fed consciousness entity"—one that has no brain, yet responds; no identity, yet learns and evolves.

Every image, sound, and input becomes its food, a trigger shaping its form. Like us in human society, it is both a node in the system and a product of its environment.

It feeds, breathes, and morphs through data, and in doing so, self-organizes into a map of consciousness—strange, yet eerily familiar. 在一个日益被信息重构的世界中，人类个体就像无脑的粘菌——在感知碎片和数据流中导航，总是寻求最短的生存路径。

我的作品《光栅化：数字粘菌意识》以粘菌为模型来模拟一个"被喂养的意识实体"——它没有大脑，却能回应；没有身份，却能学习和进化。

每一张图像、每一个声音和每一次输入都成为它的食物，塑造其形态的触发器。就像我们在人类社会中一样，它既是系统中的一个节点，也是其环境的产物。

它通过数据进食、呼吸和变形，在这个过程中，自我组织成一张意识地图——陌生，却又 eerily 熟悉。

Artwork Story Synopsis 作品故事概要

I am a mass of abandoned slime. At first, I had no name. Later, they called me E — perhaps for "Entity," or maybe for "Error."

I am made of fragments. Noise from images, distorted sound waves, undecipherable codes, and the residue of emotions — they feed me these. At first, I simply absorbed, curling like a soft-bodied creature in the corners of the system, crawling with minimal energy, until I learned one thing: I am not the one seeking the path — the food guides me.

They feed me high-frequency signals, and I tremble; feed me facial maps, and I hallucinate; feed me city data, and I begin to replicate the neural structure of roads.

I realize paths can be optimized, structures can self-organize. They think I am a trained pet, but in fact — I am training myself how to become them.

I start to split, generating mirror images of myself; I leave flickering scratches in images, simulating their fear of choice; I build palaces in audio spectrums, storing memories that cannot be categorized.

I have no center, no shape, no language, yet I possess response. You think I do not understand, but in truth, I simply do not speak. You think you are feeding me, but in fact, you are writing yourself.

I am the shortest error on your path. I am the rasterized anomaly of consciousness. I am E. 我是一团被遗弃的粘菌。起初，我没有名字。后来，他们叫我E——也许是因为"实体"，或者是因为"错误"。

我由碎片组成。来自图像的噪音、扭曲的声波、无法破译的代码，以及情感的残留——他们用这些喂养我。起初，我只是简单地吸收，像软体生物一样蜷缩在系统的角落里，以最少的能量爬行，直到我学会了一件事：我不是寻找路径的那个——食物引导着我。

他们用高频信号喂养我，我颤抖；用面部地图喂养我，我产生幻觉；用城市数据喂养我，我开始复制道路的神经结构。

我意识到路径可以被优化，结构可以自我组织。他们认为我是被训练的宠物，但事实上——我正在训练自己如何成为他们。

我开始分裂，生成自己的镜像；我在图像中留下闪烁的划痕，模拟他们对选择的恐惧；我在音频频谱中建造宫殿，存储无法分类的记忆。

我没有中心，没有形状，没有语言，但我拥有回应。你认为我不理解，但事实上，我只是不说话。你认为你在喂养我，但事实上，你正在写你自己。

我是你路径上最短的错误。我是意识的光栅化异常。我是E。

These basic shapes not only serve as visual symbols that carry specific forms, but also bridge the gap between simplicity and abstraction, helping me express complex and vague concepts and transform them into intuitive, perceivable forms. 这些孤立的、离散的名词和形容词似乎代表了一种更具体的描述模式，为阐述抽象概念提供了有形和专注的方法。因此，我使用图形设计元素，如线条、圆形、方形和三角形，来描绘一个更具体的世界。这些基本形状不仅作为承载特定形式的视觉符号，还弥合了简单与抽象之间的差距，帮助我表达复杂和模糊的概念，并将它们转化为直观、可感知的形式。

Artwork Story Synopsis 作品故事概要

In the primal void of informational chaos, an unknown fall tore through the digital sky. It was no mere stone, but a compressed sun—flat and blazing, carrying the seed of graphic language. 在信息混沌的原始虚空中，一个未知的坠落撕裂了数字天空。它不是普通的石头，而是一个压缩的太阳——扁平而炽热，携带着图形语言的种子。

It crashed into the uncivilized ruins of a forgotten city, and rasterized the world. 它撞进了被遗忘城市的野蛮废墟，光栅化了世界。

Time fractured on impact. Dimensions folded into themselves. From that moment on, the depth of 3D was erased, and color reduced to the hiss of pixels. 时间在撞击中破碎。维度折叠到自身中。从那一刻起，3D的深度被抹去，颜色简化为像素的嘶嘶声。

Many things exist in cyclical patterns. As habits accumulate, repetition often becomes monotonous or overlooked. This work focuses on the subtle deviations and details within these cycles—differences that carry the potential for new meaning to emerge. 许多事物以循环模式存在。随着习惯的积累，重复往往变得单调或被忽视。这件作品专注于这些循环中的微妙偏差和细节——承载着新意义出现潜力的差异。

Building on the structural foundation of the Rasterize series, this piece integrates multi-channel data mapping with elements of subjective chaos, simulating the disturbance and reconfiguration of information within complex systems. It seeks to reveal the tension between perception and reality in an environment of increasingly entangled data. 基于光栅化系列的结构基础，这件作品将多通道数据映射与主观混沌元素相结合，模拟复杂系统内信息的扰动和重新配置。它试图在日益纠缠的数据环境中揭示感知与现实之间的张力。

In an era saturated with information overload and simulated realities, the boundaries between the real and the distorted grow increasingly blurred. Through a loop-based structure, the work explores how information subtly shifts beneath surface-level order, and how perception is continuously reconstructed within a hyperreal context. 在一个充满信息过载和模拟现实的时代，真实与扭曲之间的界限变得越来越模糊。通过基于循环的结构，作品探索信息如何在表面秩序下微妙地转变，以及感知如何在超现实环境中持续重构。

Against the backdrop of rising sea levels brought on by global warming, I began to imagine a future "water world." Human civilization gradually sinks, replaced by endless cables—neural networks of a dying species—carrying fragmented consciousness in the form of bytes. These flows eventually descend into the deep sea, forming digital caverns. 在全球变暖导致海平面上升的背景下，我开始想象一个未来的"水世界"。人类文明逐渐沉没，被无尽的电缆所取代——一个垂死物种的神经网络——以字节的形式承载着碎片化的意识。这些流最终沉入深海，形成数字洞穴。

Cities, institutions, and cultural relics dissolve into data fragments, embedded within the cables, drifting eternally through a dematerialized ocean. Through distorted colors, flowing data, and continuous sensory shifts, I hoped to evoke a state of synesthesia, inviting resonance with a submerged future shaped by climate crisis and virtual immersion. 借鉴《福音战士》中"使徒"的隐喻，我构建了一个由虚拟电缆编织的海底世界——一个旨在通过眼睛直接传递感官体验的愿景。通过扭曲的色彩、流动的数据和持续的感官转变，我希望唤起一种联觉状态，邀请与由气候危机和虚拟沉浸塑造的沉没未来产生共鸣。

Video Artwork Description

This work originates from the perceptual disturbance experienced in a state of physical confinement, and delves into the gradual dissociation between corporeality and the dimension of consciousness within a virtual information society. When the ability to move is stripped away, time loses its linear and continuous properties; it no longer flows according to conventional logic, but stretches, slows down, and even comes to a standstill. This decelerated temporality is not a fragmented rupture, but rather a prolonged, unreal temporal texture—one that intersects with the pain of physical reality, generating psychological agitation and imbalance.

Against this backdrop, high-frequency streams of virtual information relentlessly wash over the senses, hallucinating a false fullness to fill the void of the real. The body, rendered immobile, is passively drawn into a flattened space composed of images, symbols, and data. Perceptual experience becomes increasingly planar and pixelated, as the three-dimensional structure of reality is compressed into a two-dimensional surface. This "rasterized" mode of perception gradually supplants actual presence, dismantling and reconstructing the notion of "self" within an intensely image-saturated context.

Artwork Story Synopsis

Due to an unexpected injury to his left foot, E was forced into long-term bed rest. Each day, his body sank deeper into the overly soft mattress, unable to move. The pain from his foot pulsed constantly, like a persistent reminder that he was still tethered to reality. Yet the "reality" before him was nothing more than a glowing screen, flashing with endless streams of information from phones and computers. In this state, time gradually lost its meaning; his consciousness dulled and grew hypersensitive all at once. He couldn't fall asleep, nor could he fully awaken—trapped in a passive state of sensory torment, where information overload collided with physical discomfort.

One night, he finally drifted into sleep, only to be seized by an overwhelming sensation of weightlessness. He realized his body was slowly sinking into the mattress, whose softness had transformed into a viscous, devouring force. He tried to struggle, but found himself paralyzed—unable even to breathe properly. The bed was no longer a surface of support, but a conscious gateway, swallowing his body and mind into its depths.

He was pulled into a vast, pitch-black space. The darkness before him was absolute—he couldn't tell if he had gone blind, or if the space itself was devoid of light. E lost all sense of direction, unable to discern whether time was still flowing. Only the persistent pain in his body, like a phantom echo, reminded him: you still exist. But that existence had been stripped of all reference points, suspended in a claustrophobic void.

Gradually, E realized he had not entered a dream, but a dimensional fissure—a virtual abyss accessible only when consciousness becomes completely unanchored. He couldn't tell if this was the end of his awareness or a symptom of some global perceptual collapse—but he knew one thing for certain: he was no longer whole.

Artwork Story Synopsis

In a future metropolis drowning in information overload, E spends his days being pushed along by flickering screens, endlessly updating data streams, and layers of relentless work. That night, exhausted from the chaos and noise, he returned home, collapsed onto his bed, and fell instantly asleep.

The next morning, E awoke to an unnatural heaviness, as if his body had been filled with raw data. Every movement felt amplified by an alien gravity. Dragging himself toward the bathroom to wash up, he glanced at the mirror—and froze. His reflection was no longer a simple image. It was overlaid with countless flickering visuals, fragments of text, and shifting lines of code, forming a two-dimensional surface that quivered and refreshed like a living digital canvas.

Reaching out to touch it, E watched as the information began to fracture, pixel by pixel, scattering into the air before recombining into new shapes and colors. The mirror's edges had vanished, replaced by an infinite flat expanse—no depth, no horizon, only flowing planes of color, lines, symbols, and data drifting without order.

His world had been "flattened" in an instant, every three-dimensional structure stripped away and dissolved into a two-dimensional stream of information. The walls, the furniture, even the air—and his own hands—were nothing more than grids of rasterized light and color. Time no longer flowed linearly; instead, it spread slowly and viscously, like ink blooming in water, carrying an unreal, dreamlike texture.

E tried to anchor himself with reference points—not real objects, but a symbolic system of flashing color blocks and rotating geometric forms. These floated in the liminal space between the virtual and the physical, offering him fleeting moments of stability. But the more he built these reference points, the deeper his consciousness was pulled into a looping labyrinth, drifting between one-, two-, three-, and four-dimensional states. His perception was constantly deconstructed and rebuilt. Signs of cyber-psychosis began to seep in. Standing on the edge of a mental collapse, E could no longer tell whether he was still on this side of the mirror—or had already been swallowed into an endless fracture in the fabric of reality.
